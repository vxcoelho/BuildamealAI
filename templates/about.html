About Build A Meal

Build A Meal is your AI-powered recipe companion that helps you discover and create delicious meals.

Features

  • Home: Discover featured recipes curated just for you
  • Browse Recipes: Explore our full collection of recipes
  • AI Generator: Generate custom recipes based on your ingredients
  • My Favorites: Save and organize your favorite recipes

How It Works

Simply enter the ingredients you have on hand, and our AI will generate a custom recipe tailored to your preferences, dietary restrictions, and cooking time.

Technology

Built with Flask, PostgreSQL, and deployed on Railway for seamless performance.

Happy Cooking!
